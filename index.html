Unleashing the Potential of Your Gearbox with Spline Output Shafts

At TolisGear™, we recognize the pivotal role that precision and performance play in your mechanical systems. Our gearboxes featuring spline output shafts are meticulously crafted to exceed the highest standards, ensuring unparalleled functionality and efficiency for your applications.

What Makes Spline Gearing Superior?

  • Reduced Backlash: Experience smoother and more precise motion with minimal play between components.
  • Higher Torque Transmission: Spline gears can manage greater torque, boosting the power and reliability of your machinery.
  • Compact Design: Spline shafts' lower tooth height enables more compact designs without sacrificing strength.

Understanding DIN5480 Standard

The DIN5480 standard is crucial for ensuring compatibility and precision in spline gearing. Here’s a brief overview of its key features:

  • W and N Designation: "Welle" (shaft) and "Nabe" (hub).
  • 30-degree Pressure Angle: Uniform across all DIN5480 gears, ensuring consistency.
  • Tolerance Classes: Ranging from 5 to 12, with lower numbers indicating tighter tolerances.

DIN5480 sets TolisGear™ apart from other standards like ANSI B92.2M and ISO 4156, guaranteeing unmatched precision in your applications. We utilize the most precise classes of the DIN5480 standard to deliver top-tier performance.
